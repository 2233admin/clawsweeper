Verify production Convex `GITHUB_TOKEN` and failure logs, then improve `requireGitHubAccountAge` to distinguish true rate limits from other 403 responses, surface retry/reset detail when available, and reduce repeated GitHub lookups by syncing or caching `githubCreatedAt` earlier in the auth/profile path.
