## What This Changes

The issue asks maintainers to raise anonymous and/or package download quota, or grant trusted treatment, for `@openclaw/openviking` so first-time ClawHub installs do not fail with `429 Rate limit exceeded`.

## Best Possible Solution

Maintain the existing abuse controls by default, then have a maintainer/operator review production 429 logs and the supplied OpenViking provenance details before either applying an operational trust/quota exception or adding a narrowly scoped package/publisher download quota override with tests and docs.
