## Summary

Keep this open. Current main has adjacent Telegram polling watchdogs, per-session nested lanes, timer-driven health diagnostics, and conservative stuck-session recovery, but those pieces still do not prove the reported gateway-wide freeze after nested `sessions_send` is fixed. The issue is recent, has another affected-user comment, and the cross-referenced stuck-session work covers a narrower per-session failure rather than all Telegram pollers and health output going silent.

## What This Changes

The issue reports that coordinator-to-subagent `sessions_send` activity can leave the gateway process alive while all Telegram polling, health-monitor logs, and outbound sends stop until manual restart.

## Best Possible Solution

Reproduce this on current main with multiple Telegram bot accounts and coordinator-to-subagent `sessions_send`, collect a gateway diagnostics export from an affected run, then land a focused liveness/recovery fix that proves Telegram polling and health/diagnostic timers continue after nested A2A completion or escalates to a bounded restart when they do not.
