## Summary

Keep open. Current main has dependency-backed Shift+Enter newline behavior through the pinned pi-tui editor, but OpenClaw still lacks user-facing TUI docs/help for multiline composition and has no config/CLI bridge for configurable send/newline keybindings.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open. The narrow next step is to document existing multiline composition in the public TUI guide and /help, including terminal limitations and the backslash fallback. A separate maintainer decision is needed before adding a persisted or CLI-configurable send/newline keybinding bridge into pi-tui.
