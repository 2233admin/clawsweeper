## Clownfish Work Prompt

Fix the Gemini tagged/native reasoning conflict tracked by this issue. Current main still resolves direct Google/Gemini reasoning output to `tagged` while the direct Google transport sends native Gemini `thinkingConfig.includeThoughts`, causing `<think>/<final>` prompt requirements to conflict with native thought parts and sometimes produce empty post-tool turns.

Expected boundary:
- Keep native Gemini thinking payloads working in `extensions/google/transport-stream.ts`.
- Stop direct Google AI Studio / `google-generative-ai` and Google Vertex Gemini paths from injecting tagged `<think>/<final>` requirements when native thinking is active.
- Decide deliberately whether `google-gemini-cli` should remain tagged or move to native; avoid accidentally changing it through a shared hook without tests.
- Prefer fixing the provider-owned reasoning-mode hook/family helper over adding core provider-id special cases.
- Do not change unrelated provider replay policies, tool schema cleanup, auth, or empty-payload surfacing.

Likely implementation/test paths:
- `src/plugin-sdk/provider-model-shared.ts`
- `extensions/google/provider-hooks.ts`
- `extensions/google/provider-registration.ts`
- `extensions/google/gemini-cli-provider.ts`
- `extensions/google/index.test.ts`
- `src/utils/provider-utils.test.ts`
- `extensions/google/transport-stream.test.ts`
- a focused prompt/attempt test if needed to prove `reasoningTagHint` is false for native Gemini.
