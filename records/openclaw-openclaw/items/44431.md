## Summary

Keep open. Current main implements a useful subset of the field report: selector targeting for managed Playwright act actions, batch act execution, click delay handling, stable tab/ARIA-ref guidance, and tests for normalized batch dispatch. Several requested surfaces are still missing or incomplete: no CDP inspectElement/event-listener helper, no snapshot captchaDetected signal, no refsStale/auto-refresh response signal, no post-fill verification, no waitForRef shape, and the agent tool schema/docs still do not fully expose or describe the shipped batch/selector behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open and have a maintainer split it into focused browser-plugin follow-ups. Treat selector targeting, managed-route batch actions, delayed clicks, and refs="aria" guidance as partially shipped, then scope the remaining pieces separately: inspectElement/CDP event-listener inspection, snapshot CAPTCHA detection, stale-ref signaling or auto-refresh behavior, post-fill verification or explicit slowly=true guidance, wait-by-ref semantics, and schema/docs alignment for supported act actions.
