## Summary

Keep open. Current main satisfies most of the WebChat audio request: the picker advertises audio uploads, non-image uploads are serialized through chat.send, the Gateway stages non-image media into MediaPaths/MediaTypes, and assistant audio attachments render with HTML audio controls. The remaining concrete gap is still audio/webm: the picker advertises audio/*, but the selected-file/drop helper rejects every .webm filename, and shared UI/media classification does not consistently treat .webm as audio.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open but narrow it to the remaining audio/webm work. Preserve the already-shipped WebChat audio upload/playback and Gateway MediaPaths routing behavior, then add a small tested change that accepts audio/webm without accepting video/webm, classifies audio/webm consistently as audio in WebChat/history/shared media helpers, and covers picker/drop plus rendering/routing regressions.
