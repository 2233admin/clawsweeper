- Content-based cache reuse stores evaluated binding objects from the first matching config; reviewers should confirm OpenClaw's config/bindings immutability assumptions and that identical JSON content cannot diverge in runtime-sensitive ways.
- The proposed bounded fingerprint Map is described as LRU-ish but currently behaves as FIFO on fingerprint hits unless the entry is refreshed, matching the open Greptile review comment.
