## Summary

Keep #39138 open. Current main still starts outbound delivery recovery on gateway scheduled-service activation and recovery still scans every pending delivery entry, sorts by enqueue time, and re-delivers eligible entries without a stale-age cutoff or max replay-count cap. Old retryCount=0 entries without lastAttemptAt remain immediately eligible, so the reported restart replay of many old messages remains plausible and actionable.

## Best Possible Solution

Implement a conservative outbound recovery policy in src/infra/outbound: expire or move stale pending deliveries to failed/ before replay, preserve recent crash recovery, and add focused tests for old retryCount=0 entries without lastAttemptAt, maxRecoveryMs interaction, startup recovery, and reconnect drains. If operator control is desired, add documented config/schema for max pending age and possibly max replay count.
