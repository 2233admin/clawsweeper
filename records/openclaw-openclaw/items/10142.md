## Summary

Keep open. Current main at a820a307dfeb950fa633c0ae6fb2386a35a91991 still lacks the requested internal `session:end` hook event; the adjacent `session_end` surface is a plugin hook, and open PRs #56672 and #61675 remain implementation candidates rather than merged fixes.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open until maintainers either land or close the open implementation candidates (#56672 and #61675), or make an explicit product decision that the typed plugin `session_end` hook is the supported replacement and document that in the internal hooks docs. A direct implementation should add a typed internal `session:end` event/guard, wire the relevant agent, cron, reset, and subagent completion paths, and update tests plus the public hooks docs.
