## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has the central Gemini signature-bloat path: visible Google text blocks can retain `textSignature`, same-provider replay sends that field back as Gemini `thoughtSignature`, and the replay sanitizer does not target the stored `textSignature` field. Mixed text/tool delivery now has partial protection through block flushing, but Gemini remains on tagged reasoning with strict final-tag suppression, so meaningful parts of the report remain unresolved rather than stale or implemented.

## What This Changes

The issue requests fixes for Gemini 2.5 Pro sessions bloating or failing when Gemini emits large text signatures, tagged reasoning text, and mixed text plus tool-call responses.

## Best Possible Solution

Handle this at the Google provider and replay boundary: preserve only provider-required Gemini function-call/native-thinking signatures, bound or avoid replaying stale visible-text signatures, and align Gemini reasoning delivery with native-thinking behavior so malformed or final-less tagged output cannot silently suppress user replies.
