## Summary

Keep open. ## Best Possible Solution

Keep this draft PR open for maintainer review or author rebase, but narrow it to the remaining useful deltas: prove and add a regression for chat-target-first bound-route lookup in `resolveSubagentCompletionOrigin`, and decide whether to add `message_thread_id`/thread suffixes to Telegram success logs. Do not re-land already-covered direct/queued announce fixes unless the rebase shows they are still needed.
