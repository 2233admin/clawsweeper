Keep open. The issue is fresh, corroborated by several users, and current main shows partial latency mitigations and diagnostics but no source, test, release, or live timing proof that the 4.26 Feishu/reply/reaction and first-message regression is fixed end to end.

## What This Changes

The issue reports a regression in OpenClaw 4.26 versus 4.22/4.23 where Feishu reactions, replies, and first-message handling become seconds to over a minute slower under the same configuration.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the broad upgrade-regression tracker until maintainers collect comparable latency traces across the affected versions and split any remaining proven bottlenecks into narrower fixes or canonical follow-ups such as #67000 and #73306.
