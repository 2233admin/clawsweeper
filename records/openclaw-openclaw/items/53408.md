## Summary

Keep open. Current main still does not prove the reported custom `copilot-api` / `anthropic-messages` long-session argument-loss path is fixed, and the newly closed broader report #55224 explicitly points back here as the canonical investigation. The merged Kimi and diagnostic work helps adjacent cases but does not cover a custom Anthropic-compatible provider with large fragmented `write`/`exec` arguments.

## What This Changes

The issue reports that long, tool-heavy webchat sessions can cause `write` and `exec` tool arguments to arrive with missing `content` or `command` fields, especially through a custom Anthropic-compatible `copilot-api` proxy.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the canonical bug until a maintainer captures or synthesizes a custom Anthropic-compatible stream fixture with large fragmented `write`, `exec`, and ideally `sessions_spawn` arguments, then implements provider-safe repair or explicit diagnostics without regressing Kimi-specific repair or custom endpoint beta-header handling.
