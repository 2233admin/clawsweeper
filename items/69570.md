## Summary

Current `main` still appears to generate one follow-up agent turn per approved async exec completion, route each one back into the original session, and serialize runs on the session lane. I found no read-path evidence of batching, stale-followup discard, or queued-followup cancellation, and the duplicate issue history shows `#69570` is the reporter’s intended canonical bug rather than an obsolete duplicate.
