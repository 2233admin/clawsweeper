## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a repo-side fix that strips `temperature` from Anthropic Opus 4.7 compaction traffic. The exact "hardcoded 0.2" claim is not confirmed from source: current compaction still inherits configured/global model params and forwards `temperature`, while the upstream `@mariozechner/pi-coding-agent` summarization helper in the version pinned on `main` does not appear to add `temperature` by itself. That leaves a plausible unresolved compat bug, not a high-confidence close.
