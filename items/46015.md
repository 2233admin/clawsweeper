## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has the fallback observability work and routes Discord auto-TTS through the normal provider-selection path, but the issue is not fully implemented: Discord still exposes TTS overrides only at global/account voice config scope, not per-agent voice assignment. The maintainer comment on 2026-03-31 also explicitly says the issue remains open for per-agent Discord voice assignment and provider-selection/stickiness work.
