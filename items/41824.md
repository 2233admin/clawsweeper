## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has mitigations for narration-heavy turns, but I did not find strong evidence that the reported Telegram + Claude ACP behavior is fixed. The only hard followthrough/retry contract in-tree is explicitly limited to OpenAI/OpenAI-Codex GPT-5 lanes, while Telegram still documents preview/tool-progress streaming rather than a tool-first completion gate.

## Evidence

- **Hard followthrough guard excludes the reported Claude ACP lane:** `strict-agentic` only activates for `openai`, `openai-codex`, or `mock-openai` GPT-5-family models; unsupported provider/model pairs collapse to `default`. That means the repo's explicit no-stall / planning-only retry guard does not cover the issue's `Claude Sonnet via ACP` setup.
