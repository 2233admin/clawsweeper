## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial mitigations for webchat tool visibility and interrupted-run cleanup, but the runaway-tool-call guard remains opt-in and disabled by default, so there is not strong evidence that the default regression reported in #48947 is fixed well enough to close.
