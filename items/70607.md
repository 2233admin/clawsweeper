## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` now codifies the opposite routing contract from this PR: cross-agent spawns resolve the target agent's bound account, while same-agent spawns preserve the caller's active inbound account. Merging the proposed `!==` -> `===` change would conflict with the new tests on `main` and would likely regress same-agent multi-account completion routing.
