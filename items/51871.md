## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has unguarded cron UI/CLI reads of `job.schedule.kind` / `job.payload.kind`, and the cron load/doctor paths only normalize some legacy shapes; they do not clearly eliminate jobs missing `schedule` or `payload`. That is not strong enough evidence to close as fixed or obsolete.
