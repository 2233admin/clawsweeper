## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not already contain this fix, but the proposed behavior is not cleanup-safe: preserving `schedule.expr = ""` would change direct cron service add/update paths from a hard invalid-schedule failure into an accepted enabled job with no `nextRunAtMs`. The underlying UX complaint may still be real for gateway/schema errors, but this PR needs maintainer follow-up rather than auto-close.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/gateway/protocol/schema/cron.ts:119](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca/src/gateway/protocol/schema/cron.ts#L119)
