## Summary

Keep open. Current main does not contain the reported slave-FD workaround in `createPtyAdapter`, does not carry a local `@lydell/node-pty` patch, and only cleans up JS-side PTY listeners/state. I could not find strong repo evidence that the native leak described in #47679 is resolved on `main`.

## Evidence

- **PTY adapter still spawns directly with no slave-FD close logic:** `createPtyAdapter` loads `@lydell/node-pty` and calls `spawn(...)`, then immediately continues into listener/wait setup. There is no `_fd - 1`, `tty.isatty(...)`, or `fs.closeSync(...)` workaround around the spawn path.
