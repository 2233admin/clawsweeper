## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has an Android companion app with chat, assistant entrypoints, and Tailscale-aware gateway docs, but it does not implement the requested chat-first Android surface well enough to justify closure. The request is coherent, recent, and in scope for OpenClaw rather than ClawHub.
