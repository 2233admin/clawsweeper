## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` documents and ships safer inbound channel-dispatch helpers, but the reported AgentMail handler is not present in this repository, so there is no strong evidence that issue #51168 is fixed, obsolete, or out of scope. The report is also recent and concrete, and OpenClaw's own SDK compatibility policy says existing external plugins should not break across ordinary minor releases.
