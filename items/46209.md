## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `c1383680405879a3c3f43dceb20a694f54d3c13c` does not implement the PR’s label-precedence fix: `deriveSessionTitle` still prefers `displayName`, then `subject`, then transcript `firstUserMessage`, with no `entry.label` or `entry.origin?.label` fallback before transcript text. The extracted `session-title.ts`/`session-title.test.ts` files described in the PR are also not present on `main`.
