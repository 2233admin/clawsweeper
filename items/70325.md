## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes a 10-minute timeout for non-`agentTurn` cron jobs, wraps main-session cron execution in that timeout, and waits synchronously for `runHeartbeatOnce()` when `wakeMode="now"`. Main-session jobs do not expose a configurable timeout, so the reported `cron: job execution timed out` status remains plausible on current `main` for long-running heartbeat-backed `systemEvent` jobs.

## Evidence

- **Non-agent cron jobs still default to 10 minutes:** `DEFAULT_JOB_TIMEOUT_MS` is `10 * 60_000`, and `resolveCronJobTimeoutMs()` only grants the longer/default-configurable path to `payload.kind === "agentTurn"`; other jobs keep the 10-minute ceiling.
  - file: [src/cron/service/timeout-policy.ts:8](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/af56926e2fc410809fcb0399435787c7fa8daef8/src/cron/service/timeout-policy.ts#L8)

  - file: [src/cron/service/timer.ts:79](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/af56926e2fc410809fcb0399435787c7fa8daef8/src/cron/service/timer.ts#L79)
