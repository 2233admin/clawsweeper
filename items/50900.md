## Summary

Current `main` still implements session maintenance with one global `pruneAfter` threshold only. The requested ordered per-pattern retention rules are not present in the config schema, runtime resolver, pruning path, or docs, so there is no strong basis to close this as implemented, obsolete, or out-of-scope.
