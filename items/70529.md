## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements `existing-session` attach by spawning bare `npx chrome-devtools-mcp@latest --autoConnect`, the docs/tests still describe that ambient `npx` requirement, and no inspected post-release change fixes the packaged-app `spawn npx ENOENT` variant. The provided maintainer comment also explicitly says to keep this open as the Desktop-specific variant.
