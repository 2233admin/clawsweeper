## Summary

Current `main` still lacks the Discord MIME-preservation change, and this PR's WebP fast-path would conflict with an existing shared-media contract that current tests enforce for non-Discord paths. The item should stay open for maintainer judgment or a narrower rework, not conservative auto-close.

## Evidence
