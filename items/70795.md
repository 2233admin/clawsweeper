## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial source-level coverage for unguarded `workspaceOnly=false` reads, but the reported direct-session policy mismatch is not proven fixed: the active read-tool assembly and guard remain workspace-root-centric, and there is still no regression test for the direct chat/session surface reading an approved non-workspace root.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and add a focused regression test for the direct chat/session read-tool assembly with `tools.fs.workspaceOnly=false` and an approved non-workspace readonly root. Then either thread the effective readonly roots through the topmost read-tool wrapper/tool-host, or clearly document that sandboxed direct sessions require explicit bind mounts and make the error reflect the effective policy.
