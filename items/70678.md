## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has an extension-local WhatsApp watchdog that treats lack of inbound messages as staleness and force-closes the connection after 30 minutes, so the reported quiet-session reconnect loop is still plausible and was re-confirmed in the issue discussion.

## Evidence
