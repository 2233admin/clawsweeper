## Summary

Current `main` still routes cron `delivery.mode: "announce"` replies straight to outbound delivery without the normal `maybeApplyTtsToPayload(...)` transform, so the reported `[[tts]]`/`[[tts:text]]` gap remains plausible and no conservative auto-close reason applies.

## Evidence

- **Normal reply paths still own the TTS transform:** Auto-reply delivery applies `maybeApplyTtsToPayload(...)` before outbound send in the normal reply stack, which is the behavior the issue expects cron announce to share.
