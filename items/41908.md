The core-owned parts this PR overlaps are already documented on main in `docs/plugins/message-presentation.md:11` and `docs/channels/msteams.md:727`. The detailed install/usage/reference material for `@vikrantsingh01/openclaw-adaptive-cards` should live on ClawHub and in that plugin's own repository/docs instead of as a standalone page here.
