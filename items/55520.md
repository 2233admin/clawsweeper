## Summary

Current `main` does not fully implement the requested fix. OpenAI-compatible `reasoning_content`/`reasoning` deltas are still assembled into hidden `thinking` blocks rather than promoted to visible text, `extractAssistantVisibleText()` still ignores thinking-only content, and the automatic reasoning-only recovery path is intentionally limited to strict-agentic OpenAI GPT-5 and Gemini lanes, not the reported Z.AI/Qwen-style providers.

## Evidence

- **Reasoning deltas still map to hidden thinking only:** `getCompletionsReasoningDeltas()` converts `reasoning_content`, `reasoning`, and `reasoning_text` into `{ kind: "thinking" }` entries. There is no fallback here that promotes reasoning-only output into a visible text block.
