## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the reported behavior: image support resolution is provider-scoped and fails closed when the selected provider entry is missing, and provider-model merge still overwrites explicit `input` with implicit catalog metadata. The PR is not implemented on `main`, and the attached review discussion raises a real behavior tradeoff that needs maintainer judgment rather than auto-close.
