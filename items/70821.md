## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `cc0992564bc565791810e6a560d12b430a8544ee` still does not advertise `xhigh` for Amazon Bedrock Claude Opus 4.7, while the downstream Anthropic transport already supports `xhigh` for Opus 4.7.
