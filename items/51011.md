## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not already include this iOS Privacy & Access UI or the related contacts/calendar/reminders usage-description keys, so there is no basis to close as implemented. The PR also still carries a permission-model mismatch around calendar write-only access versus the single advertised `calendar` permission bit.
