## Summary

Keep #40463 open. Current main has absorbed some Microsoft Teams attachment fixes, including hostedContent `/$value` fetching and exact `smba.trafficmanager.net` auth forwarding, but it still does not implement the PR’s channel `/chats/{threadId}` fallback or the helper-level `channelData.chatId` preference, and related DM attachment reports remain open.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #40463 open or convert it into a maintainer-cleaned follow-up: preserve the shipped `/$value` and exact SMBA auth behavior, add or explicitly reject the channel `/chats/{threadId}` fallback with tests, decide whether the helper should prefer `channelData.chatId`, use bounded response reading for hostedContent `/$value`, and coordinate any remaining DM behavior with #65329.
