## Summary

Not safe to auto-close. Current `main` intentionally keeps unauthenticated loopback probes device-less, documents unresolved gateway-auth `SecretRef` probe failures as `authWarning`, and carries tests for that behavior. This PR’s proposed change is therefore not implemented on `main`, and the discussion-raised side effects around pairing/state writes are still grounded in current code.
