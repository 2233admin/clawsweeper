## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not expose the requested active-run helpers on `openclaw/plugin-sdk/agent-runtime`, and the newer `agent-harness` surface is only a partial, experimental alternative. The PR discussion also leaves the cross-session abort capability unresolved.

## Risks / Open Questions

- Closing now would drop a request that current `main` does not clearly satisfy on the requested `plugin-sdk/agent-runtime` surface.
- Treating `plugin-sdk/agent-harness` as equivalent would require a maintainer policy judgment because it is documented as a different, experimental trusted-plugin API.
