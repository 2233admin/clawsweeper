## Summary

Keep open. Current main does not implement the PR's failure-mode-aware retry image policy, cross-provider prompt-image suppression, or fallback-threaded partial execution context. The PR is not safe to auto-close as implemented or superseded; it still targets real remaining behavior, although it likely needs a rebase and a maintainer decision on how to populate partialExecution in production.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #57959 open for maintainer review rather than auto-close it. The best path is to rebase it, preserve or split out the cross-provider prompt-image suppression and retry-image policy, and either wire partialExecution from existing embedded attempt tool metadata into FailoverError throw sites or explicitly narrow that part until the producer contract is ready.
