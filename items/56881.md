## Summary

Keep open. Current main/recent releases clearly changed the Codex transport and Discord slow-listener behavior, but the report bundles an out-of-tree `memory-lancedb-pro` regression with OpenClaw symptoms. That leaves insufficient evidence for a high-confidence close reason.
