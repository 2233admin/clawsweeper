## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a clear fix for this report, and the existing evidence is not strong enough to close it as invalid. The reported root cause is not proven by the code, but update-time mixed-version/service-state problems remain plausible, and the issue is too recent for a stale-information close.

## Evidence
