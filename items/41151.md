## Summary

Keep open: current `main` still uses `messagingToolSentMediaUrls` to strip duplicate media from the final reply, but the tool-result emission paths in `src/agents/pi-embedded-subscribe.ts` and `src/agents/pi-embedded-subscribe.handlers.tools.ts` do not populate that state. The PR targets a real unfixed gap, and the existing review comments show the submitted patch still needs follow-up rather than conservative closure.

## Evidence
