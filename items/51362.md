## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` only overlaps the PR's 404 failover handling; the session-store memory reduction, startup hook-config handling, and 15s/clamped handshake timeout are not implemented on `main`, and the proposed hook-config catch has an unresolved startup-sidecar correctness risk already called out in review.
