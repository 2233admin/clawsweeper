## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain this change, and the PR as proposed still has an unresolved functional risk for the Logs tab on busy gateways, so there is no strong close reason.

## Risks / Open Questions

- The reported UI-sluggishness concern may still be real, but this PR's 5 s logs cadence can regress live tail behavior under high log volume.
- A narrower follow-up could still be viable, such as reducing Nodes/Debug polling only or making Logs polling adaptive/backpressure-aware instead of uniformly slower.
