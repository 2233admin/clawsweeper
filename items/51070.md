## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` intentionally rejects loopback-source `trusted-proxy` auth, has explicit tests for that fail-closed behavior, and documents same-host loopback proxies as unsupported. This PR is not implemented on `main`, and the security/product direction appears unresolved rather than obsolete.
