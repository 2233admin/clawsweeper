## Summary

Current `main` substantially overlaps the PR's shutdown-race work, but it does not clearly implement the PR's full scope. In particular, the new Discord lifecycle stack on `main` now routes abort/disconnect through `waitForDiscordGatewayStop()` and suppresses late `reconnect-exhausted` events during teardown, but there is no corresponding `metrics` listener refreshing `lastEventAt` during healthy idle periods. Because only part of the PR is clearly superseded, the evidence is not strong enough to close conservatively.
