## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to have fixed the duplicate `action_id` and block-delivery/streaming bypass parts of the report, but the interaction path still only enqueues a system event without triggering a run, and Slack interaction session-key resolution still ignores `threadTs`, so threaded button/select flows are not fully fixed end-to-end.
