## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has related fixes for reset-hook workspace scoping and Feishu conversation binding reuse, but I did not find strong enough evidence that the exact two reported duplicate-workspace flows in #49071 are fully resolved end-to-end on current `main`.

## Risks / Open Questions

- The `/new` side of the report appears partially addressed, but the issue has two distinct scenarios and only one maps directly to a shipped changelog fix.
- I did not reproduce the Feishu config-to-live transition against current main, and there is no direct regression test covering that exact path.
- Closing now could hide a still-real Feishu/session-routing bug if the duplicate workspace is caused by a narrower live-session binding edge case rather than the reset-hook leak.
