## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` intentionally streams summarized tool/progress activity into Discord preview edits by default when preview streaming is active, and the docs/config surface expose that behavior rather than indicating it was fixed. The report is therefore not already implemented/obsolete, and changing Discord to keep those updates fully internal would need explicit maintainer product/security judgment.

## Evidence

- **Discord preview streaming renders tool/progress lines into the live draft:** `previewToolProgressEnabled` is derived from channel config, and `pushPreviewToolProgress()` builds a visible `Working…` preview with bullet lines and sends it to the draft stream.
