## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes health-monitor recovery as `stopChannel` -> `resetRestartAttempts` -> `startChannel`, and the config/docs surface does not expose any `channelHealthRestartMode` or per-channel `healthMonitor.restartMode` option.

## Evidence

- **Health monitor still does full reset+restart:** `runCheck()` logs a restart, then stops the channel, unconditionally calls `resetRestartAttempts`, and starts the channel again. There is no branch for a soft/graceful reconnect mode.
  - file: [src/gateway/channel-health-monitor.ts:156](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7e49cc87f9e9cdad47d0572331d7a7a47281a83e/src/gateway/channel-health-monitor.ts#L156)
