## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has provider-gated `<final>` enforcement plumbing, but it does not expose `agents.defaults.enforceFinalTag` in the config schema/docs and does not wire a config override into reply/runner behavior. This PR's requested surface is therefore not implemented on `main`.
