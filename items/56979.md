## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still logs every auth-failure state update and every model-fallback decision at `warn`, and the only new suppression on `main` applies to console probe lines, not file logging. That means the reported `gateway.err.log` spam remains plausible on `3a64aa49a9014518fa1977fbc023f3e0b978ae5d` even though cooldown windows themselves are now kept immutable.
