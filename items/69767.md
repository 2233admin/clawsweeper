## Summary

Keep open. Current main mitigates one related symptom by falling back to direct delivery when the announce-agent turn returns no visible payload, but the exact fail-closed behavior requested here is still missing: the completion flow can still deliver a `(no output)` completion when no child result payload was captured.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #69767 open and implement a narrow subagent completion follow-up: when `expectsCompletionMessage` is true and no non-silent child result, structured delivery payload, or sanitized fallback is available, leave the cleanup/announce retryable instead of sending `(no output)`. Preserve the current direct channel fallback for announce-agent empty-payload responses, and consider landing or extracting the relevant fail-closed pieces from #68464.
