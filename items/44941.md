## Summary

Current main partially addresses the theme with verbose-mode fallback and recovery visibility, but it does not satisfy #44941’s central request for zero-config/default-on per-session or per-topic user notifications. The related open items also do not cleanly supersede it because #30245 is fallback-only/opt-in and #44935 is broader recovery behavior.
