## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still throws the cited local-media allowlist error, the delivery queue still treats non-abort live send failures as ordinary retries, and the permanent-error classifier/tests do not include this case or the proposed `message is too long` case. I did not find evidence that the requested queue-classification change is already implemented.

## Evidence
