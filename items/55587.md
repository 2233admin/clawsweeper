## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `d3d9b5738f83d92787f57db9186ddcebda60af9a` still marks any non-zero `launchctl print` as `missingUnit`, and the status CLI still turns that into `Service unit not found` plus `openclaw gateway install` guidance while independently reading and printing the LaunchAgent plist command/path. The reported contradictory macOS status output remains consistent with current code.

## Evidence
