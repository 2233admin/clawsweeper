## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hard-codes cron run-log summaries to 2000 characters, `cron.runLog` still only exposes prune-by-bytes and keep-lines controls, and `openclaw cron runs` returns the JSONL-backed run-log entries directly. The reported truncation remains real on `main` and is not implemented or obsoleted.
