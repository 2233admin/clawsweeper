## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not add `--thread-id` to `openclaw cron add/edit`, and `cron edit` still does a single-page `cron.list` lookup even though `cron.list` is capped at 200 jobs. The maintainer’s March 30 review identified that pagination bug and asked for a fresh replacement PR; the follow-up PR #60373 was later closed unmerged, and the underlying feature request #50982 is still open. That means this PR is neither implemented on `main` nor safely closeable under the allowed auto-close reasons.
