## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR’s fix, and the stale-snapshot restart bug still appears plausible from the shipped code: `globalVersion` still starts at `0`, snapshots still persist that version, and the refresh checks in both session paths will not rebuild a cached `version: 0` snapshot when the next in-memory version is also `0` after restart.
