## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not include this PR's active-handler watchdog bypass or pre-handler middleware hook in the Telegram plugin, and the docs still describe false polling-stall restarts as something users may need to mitigate by tuning `pollingStallThresholdMs`.
