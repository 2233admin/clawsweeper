## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement transcript-level `.jsonl` rotation or expose the proposed `session.maintenance.transcriptRotateBytes` / `transcriptMaxLines` config, and the PR discussion shows the branch has addressed multiple concrete correctness/performance review findings rather than becoming obsolete.
