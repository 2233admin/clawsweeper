## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` only carries the `tools.web.fetch.ssrfPolicy.allowRfc2544BenchmarkRange` subset. The other requested surfaces in PR #55485 are still missing on `main`: no `tools.web.search.citationRedirect.ssrfPolicy` config/runtime plumbing, no `messages.remoteMedia.ssrfPolicy` config/runtime plumbing, and browser config still does not expose or forward `allowRfc2544BenchmarkRange`. This is not implemented-on-main, not obsolete, and not safe to close as maintainer cleanup.
