## Summary

Keep open. The report is recent and plausible, and current main does not show a clear fix: WhatsApp inbound filtering still only excludes `@status` and `@broadcast`, same-phone DMs are still explicitly authorized, and I did not find a Meta AI-specific guard or regression test in the WhatsApp plugin.
