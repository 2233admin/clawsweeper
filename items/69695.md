## Summary

Current `main` does not appear to fix this. The Linux systemd service probe still throws on `systemctl --user is-enabled` bus failures, still classifies `No medium found` as a user-bus-unavailable condition, and still ships WSL2 guidance that assumes systemd is not enabled. That matches the reported symptom more than an obsolete report, so this is not a safe auto-close.
