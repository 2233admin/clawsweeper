## Summary

Current `main` still has the reported hot-path structure in `sessions.list`: it builds full gateway rows before applying `search`, `activeMinutes`, and `limit`, still performs per-row transcript fallback reads when usage fields are missing, and still resolves child sessions by scanning the whole store for each row. I found no landed fix on this checkout.

## Evidence

- **Limit still applied after full row build:** `listSessionsFromStore()` filters cheap key/entry predicates, then immediately maps every candidate through `buildGatewaySessionRow()` at lines 1528-1538, sorts, and only later applies `search`, `activeMinutes`, and `limit` at lines 1542-1558. That matches the reported "full row build before filter/limit" behavior.
