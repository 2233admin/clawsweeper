## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still dual-binds loopback on `127.0.0.1` and `::1` whenever `::1` is bindable, with no Windows-specific guard. That matches the issue’s reported failure mode and the linked PR #69701 being an open fix rather than shipped behavior.

## Evidence
