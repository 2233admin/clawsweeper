## Summary

Current `main` at `d3f6783b16ecc10c67b101bf019ca4294453206a` does not include the requested `audio/webm`, `video/webm`, or `video/x-matroska` mappings, and the active outbound-media code still relies on `extensionForMime(...)` to derive attachment filenames and stored media IDs. This PR appears to address a real, still-unfixed gap rather than something already implemented on `main`.

## Evidence

- **Missing MIME mappings on main:** `EXT_BY_MIME` on current `main` includes `audio/mp4`, `video/mp4`, and `video/quicktime`, but not `audio/webm`, `video/webm`, or `video/x-matroska`.

  - file: [src/media/mime.ts:166](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/d3f6783b16ecc10c67b101bf019ca4294453206a/src/media/mime.ts#L166)
