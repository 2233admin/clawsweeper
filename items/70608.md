## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes the WhatsApp Web idle watchdog to 30 minutes, uses that watchdog to force a reconnect, and does not expose any documented user config to tune or disable it. The report is coherent, recent, and matches shipped behavior on `main`.

## Evidence

- **Hardcoded 30-minute watchdog default:** `monitorWebChannel` sets `messageTimeoutMs` to `tuning.messageTimeoutMs ?? 30 * 60 * 1000` and `watchdogCheckMs` to `tuning.watchdogCheckMs ?? 60 * 1000`, so the idle timeout is still fixed at 30 minutes unless an internal caller injects a test/runtime override.
  - file: [extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply/monitor.ts:183](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/0e9156d2053022d208c3eea1d0fa1e377ed0a154/extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply/monitor.ts#L183)
  - command: `nl -ba extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply/monitor.ts | sed -n '120,210p'`
  - sha: [0e9156d20530](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/0e9156d2053022d208c3eea1d0fa1e377ed0a154)
