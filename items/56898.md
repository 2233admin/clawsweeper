## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still ships `wacli` as a bundled core skill, and its safety section only has minimal soft guidance. The requested recipient allowlist, audit logging, attachment/bulk-send confirmation details, and stronger enforcement are not implemented in the current `skills/wacli/SKILL.md`. This also does not cleanly fit `clawhub`: OpenClaw still bundles `wacli`, and `VISION.md` explicitly prioritizes security/safe defaults for bundled skills.
