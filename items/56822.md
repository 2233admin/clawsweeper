## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the proposed `sessions_await` tool, does not expose `suppressAnnounce`/`waitForCompletion` on `sessions_spawn`, and still documents sub-agent completion as push-based auto-announce rather than a blocking fan-in primitive.
