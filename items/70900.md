## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the `surface_error` throw guarded only by `!externalAbort && !timedOut`, while the failover decision path can still reach `surface_error` from a non-null classified `failoverReason` even when `failoverFailure` is false. The PR’s proposed `&& params.failoverFailure` gate is not present on `main`, and the current tests do not cover the stale-classified-success case described in the PR.

## Evidence

That means a classified reason alone can still convert the turn into a hard error.
