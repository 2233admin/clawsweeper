## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has not already shipped this optimization, and the proposed snapshot-path swap would change behavior: plugin compatibility notices are derived from hook registrations, while metadata-only snapshot loads skip module loading and therefore do not populate those hook registrations.

## Evidence

- Auto-closing it would discard a real performance opportunity that still needs a narrower, behavior-preserving implementation.
