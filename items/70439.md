## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has the shared audio-preflight helper and recent Signal media-type fixes, but Signal still does not invoke preflight transcription or expose a Signal audio-preflight config/hook. The 2026-04-25 maintainer comment in the issue matches the code: partially addressed, not closed.
