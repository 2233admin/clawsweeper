## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still installs Fish completion by editing `~/.config/fish/config.fish` rather than placing `openclaw.fish` in Fish's auto-loaded completions directory, so #26428 is not implemented on `d3f6783b16ecc10c67b101bf019ca4294453206a` and not shipped in `v2026.4.22` either.
