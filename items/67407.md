## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has already moved config/auth/plugin startup work ahead of the bind step, and the cited context-engine `info.id` mismatch regression is covered on `main`, but the requested feature itself is still not implemented: there is no `openclaw gateway preflight` subcommand or equivalent no-bind startup-validation/update gate.
