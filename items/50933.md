## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still limits bundled session-memory to manual `/new` and `/reset` hooks, and the stale-session rollover path does not emit any `session:idle_reset` or `session:daily_reset` internal event. The PR is still targeting missing behavior on `main`, not something already shipped or obsolete.
