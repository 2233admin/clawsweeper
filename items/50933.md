## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still rotates stale sessions for daily/idle expiry, but it does not emit internal `session:idle_reset` or `session:daily_reset` events, and the bundled `session-memory` hook is still wired only to manual `command:new`/`command:reset` resets.

## Evidence
