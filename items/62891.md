## Summary

Keep this issue open. The reporterâ€™s comments narrow the report to a cron/channel readiness race, and current main still starts cron after channel startup handoff rather than after Discord-style provider connectivity. Cron startup catch-up and hot cron reload can execute work while configured channels are still in startup grace or disconnected.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open and implement a bounded cron readiness gate at the gateway/cron boundary. The likely change belongs around `activateGatewayScheduledServices` and hot cron reload in `server-reload-handlers.ts`: delay startup catch-up and automatic timer execution for channel-delivered cron work until the relevant configured channels are connected/healthy, a configurable wait expires, or readiness fails with clear diagnostics. Add targeted coverage for full startup, config.patch hot reload, SIGUSR1 restart, Discord-style asynchronous readiness, missed-job catch-up, and no-channel or webhook-only cron jobs.
