## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still serves the `health` endpoint from `getHealthSnapshot()`, and that path builds Telegram summaries from a synthetic snapshot that only sets `accountId`, `enabled`, and `configured`. Telegram’s `buildChannelSummary` then defaults missing lifecycle and credential-source fields, so `running` becomes `false` and `tokenSource` becomes `"none"` even when the runtime bot is actually up.

## Evidence
