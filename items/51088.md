## Summary

Not strong enough to close. Current `main` already supports iMessage acknowledgments through the recommended `bluebubbles` channel, but the issue specifically asks for native acknowledgment on the legacy `imessage`/`imsg` channel, and that channel still does not expose reaction support or iMessage-specific read-receipt config.

## Evidence

- **Legacy iMessage is still the separate deprecated path:** The docs still describe `imessage` as the legacy `imsg` integration and explicitly direct new iMessage setups to BlueBubbles instead of claiming feature parity.
  - file: [docs/channels/imessage.md:9](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca/docs/channels/imessage.md#L9)

There is no `reactions` capability or message-action surface here, which is the core gap for `ackReactionScope`/tapback-style acknowledgment on `channels.imessage`.
  - file: [extensions/imessage/src/shared.ts:82](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca/extensions/imessage/src/shared.ts#L82)
