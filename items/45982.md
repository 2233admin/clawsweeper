## Summary

Current `main` does not show the reported fix in `memory-lancedb`. The plugin still sends embedding requests through the OpenAI SDK without `encoding_format: "float"`, while its config/docs still advertise OpenAI-compatible `baseUrl` usage such as `http://localhost:11434/v1`. I did not find evidence on `main` that this issue was implemented or made obsolete.
