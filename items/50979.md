## Summary

Current `main` does not already implement this Slack message-tool formatting guidance, and the PR's latest direction still has an unresolved scope problem: plain-text Slack sends are Markdown-converted, but Slack block/presentation sends are passed through as raw mrkdwn. That means the item is still actionable and should stay open rather than be auto-closed.
