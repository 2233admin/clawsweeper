## Summary

Current main appears to fix only part of the report: legacy `before_agent_start` is now computed once and reused, but OpenClaw still emits the separate `message_received`, `after_compaction`, and `session_start` hook surfaces, and `tool_result_persist` context still does not carry a workspace directory/cwd field. That is not strong enough evidence to close this as fully implemented or obsolete.
