## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not expose a Discord `webhook-create` action through the shared message tool, and the request is recent, specific, and still actionable.

## Evidence

- **Message-tool discovery omits webhook creation:** Discord message-tool discovery on `main` enumerates actions such as `thread-create`, `channel-create`, and `category-create`, but there is no `webhook-create` action in the exported action list.
