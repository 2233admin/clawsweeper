## Summary

Keep #43775 open. Current main still handles a truly newer `meta.lastTouchedVersion` with only the original warning path; it does not hard-error, auto-downstamp, persist a repair, or add the prominent remediation requested for the reported silent Discord/OpenAI-Codex delivery failure. The exact fix PR #46336 was closed unmerged, while #70743 appears to address adjacent GPT-5.4/follow-up delivery hardening rather than the config-version-stamp mismatch itself.
