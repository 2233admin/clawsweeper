## Summary

Current `main` does not contain either proposed fix from PR #57608. Google Chat auth failures still discard `verification.reason` without logging it, and Zalo still defaults `mediaMaxMb` to 5 MB across code, tests, and docs. Under the requested conservative cleanup policy, there is no strong basis to close this PR.
