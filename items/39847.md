## Summary

Keep #39847 open. Current main has newer mitigation that strips copied inbound metadata from normal user-facing assistant text and Pi replay history, but the specific shared outbound delivery safety net requested here is still missing: `createOutboundPayloadPlanEntry()` still parses raw `payload.text`, and `normalizeReplyPayloadsForDelivery()` remains only a projection wrapper over that plan. Direct channel delivery normalization only runs channel text sanitizers such as HTML/plain-text cleanup, not `stripInboundMetadata()`.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #39847 as the canonical bug until maintainers either land a focused source-level fix in `src/infra/outbound/payloads.ts`/final delivery projection with regression tests for metadata plus directive/media preservation, or explicitly decide that the newer `sanitizeUserFacingText()` mitigation is the intended boundary and close the open duplicate fix PRs with that rationale.
