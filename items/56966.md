## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` intentionally treats Telegram pairing approvals as DM-only and requires explicit config allowlists for group command authorization, so the reported behavior still exists by design. That makes the issue more like a product/security decision than a clearly obsolete bug, and none of the allowed close reasons fit confidently.
