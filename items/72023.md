## Summary

Keep #72023 open. The checked-out main at 43a003b8 still reconciles pending ACP identities without skipping `mode: "oneshot"`, so the linked startup warning remains reachable for the records described in #72013. The PR head adds the focused one-shot skip and regression test, and the earlier dead `state === "closed"` concern was addressed by dropping that part of the change.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #72023 open and land it, or an equivalent maintainer patch, that skips `session.acp.mode === "oneshot"` before startup pending-identity reconciliation and keeps the focused regression test. Do not close as implemented unless main gains that explicit startup skip or another source-level change demonstrably prevents historical one-shot pending records from reaching the startup warning path.
