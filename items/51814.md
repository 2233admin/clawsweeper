## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has restart-specific recovery seams, but not the general native pending-task persistence and auto-resume after arbitrary gateway restart described in #51814. System events are explicitly ephemeral, restart recovery is driven by a single prewritten `restart-sentinel.json`, and the only automatic post-restart resume logic I found is limited to subagent orphan recovery.
