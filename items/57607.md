## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` and the latest release still drop Mattermost group messages from non-allowlisted senders before pending history is recorded, so the reported behavior is still present and not strongly closeable as fixed, obsolete, or incoherent.

## Evidence

- **Current main still returns on `GROUP_POLICY_NOT_ALLOWLISTED` before history recording:** In the Mattermost monitor, non-allowlisted group senders are dropped immediately at the access-check branch. The `recordPendingHistory` helper is defined later, after this early return.

  - sha: [7e49cc87f9e9](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/7e49cc87f9e9cdad47d0572331d7a7a47281a83e)
- **Pending history is only recorded on later mention/onchar gating drops:** `recordPendingHistoryEntryIfEnabled` is called through `recordPendingHistory()` for `onchar-not-triggered` and `missing-mention`, but not for the earlier non-allowlisted sender branch.
  - file: [extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/monitor.ts:1394](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7e49cc87f9e9cdad47d0572331d7a7a47281a83e/extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/monitor.ts#L1394)
