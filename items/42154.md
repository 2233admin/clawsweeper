## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR's requested behavior of carrying top-level `tools.alsoAllow` / `agents.list[].tools.alsoAllow` plugin entries through the later sandbox filter. Instead, `main` has a separate sandbox-scoped additive knob (`tools.sandbox.tools.alsoAllow` / `agents.list[].tools.sandbox.tools.alsoAllow`) and documents that sandbox policy as a distinct layer. That makes the PR's proposed semantics still materially different from current `main`, so there is not strong evidence to close it as already implemented or obsolete.
