## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not support `block`/`blockReason` on `before_model_resolve`, and the runtime continues after hook failures. The issue describes a real SDK gap rather than something already implemented or ClawHub-only; the linked budget-guard plugin PRs in the issue timeline are a concrete downstream consumer of that gap.
