## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` clearly addresses the macOS LaunchAgent recovery path, and the doctor-side memory-provider check now looks non-fatal, but the issue bundles multiple behaviors and I did not find strong evidence that the cron notification/delivery mismatch or package-manager auto-upgrade failure mode are fully resolved end to end.
