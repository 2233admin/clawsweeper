## Summary

Current `main` only emits the internal Telegram `message:sent` hook for `preview-finalized` DM replies. It does not implement this PR's broader `message_sent` plugin-hook coverage or its `preview-retained` cases, and the PR discussion surfaced correctness risks in those retained/regressive branches. This is not strong enough to auto-close as already implemented.
