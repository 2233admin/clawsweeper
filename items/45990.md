## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `c1383680405879a3c3f43dceb20a694f54d3c13c` does not implement this PR's per-round embedded `llm_input`/`llm_output` hook behavior. The embedded runner still emits `llm_input` before `activeSession.prompt(...)` and `llm_output` once after the attempt completes, while the message-stream handlers do not emit hook callbacks on assistant `message_start`/`message_end`. The proposed `llmInputRoundCount` field is also absent on `main`.
