## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has new malformed-tool-argument repair and loop-breaker defenses, but there is not high-confidence evidence that the reporter's specific `"{}"` parameter contamination on a local GLM/OpenAI-compatible backend is fully fixed.

The follow-up comment only says the problem reproduces across March/April versions.
