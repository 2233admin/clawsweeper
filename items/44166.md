## Summary

Keep #44166 open. Current main still routes `embedBatchWithRetry()` through a retry policy that only recognizes rate-limit/429/5xx/Cloudflare/resource-exhausted style messages, so the reported transient transport errors such as `fetch failed`, `ECONNRESET`, `socket hang up`, `terminated`, and `other side closed` are still not treated as retryable. There is also no recursive split fallback after retry exhaustion. The linked PR #44167 directly targets this issue but is still open/unmerged, and #59137 is related recovery work that explicitly does not claim to stop `fetch failed` failures.
