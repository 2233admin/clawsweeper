## Summary

Keep #71656 open. Current main has partial fixes for session-corpus noise and cursor draining, and deep promotion ranking is weighted/recency-aware, but the reported flat ingestion scores are still present: daily snippets enter at 0.62 and session-corpus snippets enter at 0.58 before promotion ranking. The related open items do not fully cover this issue’s combined corpus filtering, content-quality scoring, and scan-rotation scope.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and narrow it into concrete memory-core work: add content-type-aware scoring before `recordShortTermRecalls` for daily and session ingestion, extend corpus dedupe/filtering for repeated low-signal blocks and test/status chatter, and add regression coverage proving session-corpus ingestion continues across files and days under budget pressure. Link #57307 for the broader importance/time-decay policy and #68876 for the narrower corpus-noise filtering PR without treating either as a full duplicate.
