## Summary

Keep open. Current main implements an important subset of the field report, especially selector targeting, lower-level /act batch requests, delayed clicks, wait-by-selector, and aria-ref support. The merged follow-up PRs therefore help, but they do not supersede the whole seven-item issue: there is still no inspectElement/CDP event-listener action, no captchaDetected or refsStale response signal, no waitForRef field, no post-fill verification, and the browser agent tool schema still does not expose batch as an act kind.
