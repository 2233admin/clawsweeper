## Summary

Keep open. The related closed PR #69643 is the prior attempt that this PR re-opened, not a shipped or canonical replacement.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #70498 open and merge or manually apply its Environment variables section to `docs/channels/zalouser.md`, with wording aligned to the current `resolveProfile` and QR login precedence. That is the narrow maintainer path for closing the Zalo Personal env-var documentation gap tracked by #65864 and the earlier closed #69643 attempt.
