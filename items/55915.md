## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still documents remote mobile/gateway access through Tailscale or another direct `wss://` endpoint, and the only relay implemented in docs/config is the APNs push relay for official iOS builds. I found no `main` evidence for the proposed cloud login, gateway registration/heartbeat, or WebSocket relay transport, so this is not `implemented_on_main`; it is also too recent and too concrete to close as stale or incoherent.
