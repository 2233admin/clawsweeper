## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a general Mattermost stale-socket/deaf-websocket fix. The Mattermost monitor only adds a bot `update_at` poll and reconnects when that account metadata changes, while the generic health monitor only triggers `stale-socket` when a channel reports `lastTransportActivityAt`. Mattermost does not currently publish that transport-liveness signal, so the reported condition is not strongly shown as fixed on `main`.

## Evidence
