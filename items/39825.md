## Summary

Keep #39825 open. Current main still has the reported Discord native voice join failure shape: `/vc join` waits for `VoiceConnectionStatus.Ready` with a fixed 15s timeout, destroys the connection on readiness failure, and only records the session after that wait succeeds, so `/vc status` can still report `No active voice sessions.` after an abort. The issue discussion also includes newer-build reproductions and a maintainer comment explicitly saying to keep it open.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #39825 open until the bundled Discord plugin fixes or deliberately supersedes the voice readiness abort path. A maintainer fix likely belongs in extensions/discord/src/voice/manager.ts with regression coverage for `entersState` aborts/timeouts before session activation, clearer user/operator diagnostics, and either bounded retry/backoff or a documented connect-timeout control if that is the chosen product behavior. Related newer reports such as #63098 can stay linked as corroborating field signal rather than replacing this issue unless maintainers choose a single canonical tracker.
