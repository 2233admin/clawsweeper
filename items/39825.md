## Summary

Keep #39825 open. Current main still has the same Discord voice readiness-abort behavior shape described by the issue: `/vc join` creates a connection, waits up to a hard-coded 15 seconds for `VoiceConnectionStatus.Ready`, destroys the connection on failure, and records the session only after that wait succeeds. That means an `entersState` abort/timeout can still produce `Failed to join voice channel: The operation was aborted` followed by `/vc status` reporting no active sessions. The newer #63098 is related and open, but the supplied context does not show a maintainer decision making it the canonical replacement, while a maintainer comment on #39825 explicitly recommended keeping this issue open based on fresh field signal.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #39825 open as the Discord native voice readiness-abort tracker unless maintainers intentionally consolidate it into #63098. A concrete fix likely belongs in `extensions/discord/src/voice/manager.ts`: add regression coverage for `entersState` aborts/timeouts before session activation, improve diagnostics around readiness failure, and decide whether to add bounded retry/backoff, a longer default, or a documented `channels.discord.voice.connectTimeoutMs` setting.
