## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still appears to have the reported bug: `isRunnableJob` has a `skipAtIfAlreadyRan` guard for completed `kind: "at"` jobs, but the normal scheduler paths (`onTimer` and `runDueJobs`) still call `collectRunnableJobs(...)` without enabling that guard, while startup catch-up does enable it. That matches the issue report and the maintainer diagnosis in the thread, so there is not strong evidence to close this as already fixed or obsolete.
