## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` added a provider-specific `reasoning_content` replay fix for DeepSeek, but the Kimi/Moonshot paths still do not show analogous handling for thinking-enabled tool-call replay, so there is not strong evidence that #70563 is already fixed or obsolete.

## Evidence
