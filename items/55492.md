## Summary

Current `main` does not implement the requested unified pre-execution planner or an `openclaw route` surface. The relevant behavior is still spread across prompt instructions, ACP/subagent docs, approval guidance, and separate skill-loading paths. `VISION.md` also treats heavy orchestration / planner-tree architecture as a roadmap guardrail rather than a settled duplicate or plugin-only case, so this needs explicit maintainer product judgment instead of conservative auto-close.
