## Summary

Current `main` appears to have fixed the quote-reply half of #50944, but outbound `@mention` support is still missing in the WhatsApp send path. The issue should stay open because the reported request was for both quote-replies and mentions, and only the quote plumbing is implemented on `main`.

## Evidence

- **Reply-to IDs now flow into WhatsApp outbound quoting:** `createWhatsAppOutboundBase()` resolves `replyToId` into a `quotedMessageKey` and forwards it on both text and media sends.
  - file: [extensions/whatsapp/src/outbound-base.ts:120](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca/extensions/whatsapp/src/outbound-base.ts#L120)
  - sha: [6944d7025d9c](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca)
- **Quoted Baileys options are built and passed to sendMessage:** `buildQuotedMessageOptions()` creates a Baileys `quoted` payload, and `createWebSendApi().sendMessage()` passes that as the third argument to `sock.sendMessage()` when `quotedMessageKey` is present.
  - file: [extensions/whatsapp/src/inbound/send-api.ts:78](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca/extensions/whatsapp/src/inbound/send-api.ts#L78)
  - sha: [6944d7025d9c](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca)
- **Quote support is covered by current tests:** Tests assert that outbound WhatsApp sends preserve `quotedMessageKey` and pass the quoted remote JID through to Baileys.
  - file: [extensions/whatsapp/src/inbound/send-api.test.ts:222](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca/extensions/whatsapp/src/inbound/send-api.test.ts#L222)

  - file: [extensions/whatsapp/src/inbound/types.ts:12](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6944d7025d9c0ba4891cbe646ab5dab039d504ca/extensions/whatsapp/src/inbound/types.ts#L12)
