## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes outbound payload text through `splitMediaFromOutput`, and that function still collapses multi-space runs and trims token-cleaned lines, so indentation-sensitive body text after `MEDIA:` or `[[audio_as_voice]]` stripping is not clearly fixed on `main`. The directive parser has improved and now covers some reply-tag indentation cases, but the PR's remaining media/audio-path bug is still plausible and not proven implemented.
