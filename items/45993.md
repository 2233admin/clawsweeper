## Summary

Current `main` does not implement the requested cross-platform `openclaw migrate export` / `openclaw migrate import` workflow. OpenClaw ships `backup create` and `backup verify`, and the migration guide still documents a manual tar/copy process plus `openclaw doctor` on the target machine.
