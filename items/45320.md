## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes Discord preview-message rotation to `block` mode, does not expose an independent assistant-boundary split option, and does not include the PR’s draft-stream hardening. The PR is also stale against the current nested `channels.discord.streaming` config shape and has an unresolved review concern about superseded send failures during rotation.
